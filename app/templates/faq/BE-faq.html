Buying

Looking for a book or other item? You can search for them using the Search function at the top page after signing in with your Bethel Account. Once you find the item you're looking for, clicking on it will bring you to a page with the seller's contact information (as well as a list of other items that he or she is selling). You can contact the seller by phone or email and arrange the details of the purchase. BSG also provides a convenient drop off spot for books in the Loft (BC400). If you choose to use the Loft as a drop off location, place a post-it note with the date of drop off, seller's name, and buyer's name and email. The Loft receptionist will then store your item until pick up and will email your recipient to notify them that the item is ready for pick up. Please note that the Loft will not handle any money. Exchanging money will have to be sorted out through POs or another means of exchange.

Selling

Before you list anything that you want to sell, be sure to update your contact information, as this information is used by potential buyers who want to contact you. You can then post your items on the Exchange. Please remember to remove items you have sold so people will not try to contact you about an item that you no longer have.

Old Items

BSG has implemented a policy that allows the Exchange to continue to run smoothly by removing items that are over 6 months old. When one of your books is placed on the expired list, you will receive an email.
